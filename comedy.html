Top 10 best comedy movies

If your friends are not around or they don't want to get out of the house, what else would be better than a comedy movie. You know, that movie you've heard about but didn't have the time to watch it. That movie that everyone talks about. Well, get your popcorn, your fluffy cat and turn on the tv because it's time to laugh./p>
